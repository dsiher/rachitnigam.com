Biography

Rachit Nigam is a PhD student studying computer science at Cornell University. He is a part of the PL@Cornell and CAPRA research groups and is advised by Adrian Sampson. He leads the Dahlia project and is interested tools and languages for hardware design. He previously worked in the PLASMA research group at the University of Massachusetts Amherst with Arjun Guha.
